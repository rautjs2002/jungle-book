Summary:

Tabaqui, the jackal, serves as a sycophant and scavenger in The Jungle Book. He is generally disliked by other animals due to his cunning and untrustworthy nature. Tabaqui often aligns himself with Shere Khan, the tiger, and acts as his informant, spreading fear and chaos among the other animals in the jungle.
